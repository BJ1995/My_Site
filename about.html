Our 6-D Process

Preparation

We build your security posture to attain resilience against cyber attack of any kind.

Monitoring

We understand that security is a journey, not a destination. Therefore we keep your infrastructure in check against cyber criminals.

Detection

Within your network, We detect activities that are not in compromise with your company's security policy.

containment

When an event is marked as true positive, we take full control of the event by blocking from spreading across other systems.

Recorvery

By performing a root cause analysis on the event, we bring the system back to a safe state.

Post-Recorvery

We review the timeline of the incident and the effectiveness of response actions and make improvements where necessary.
